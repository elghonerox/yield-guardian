## 3. Specific Use Case: Autonomous Multi-Protocol Yield Optimizer

### Problem Statement

**Current Pain Points:**
1. DeFi users manually check yields across protocols (time-consuming)
2. Yields change frequently, requiring constant monitoring
3. Users struggle to identify best risk-adjusted opportunities
4. Manual reallocation is slow and misses opportunities
5. No autonomous solution that considers both yield and risk

**Market Size:**
- **TAM:** All DeFi users seeking yield (millions)
- **SAM:** Active yield farmers (hundreds of thousands)
- **SOM:** Users willing to pay for optimization (tens of thousands)

### Solution: YieldGuardian

**Core Function:**
Autonomous AI agent that continuously monitors yields across multiple DeFi protocols, analyzes risk-adjusted returns, and automatically reallocates assets to maximize yields while maintaining user-defined risk profiles.

**Key Capabilities:**
1. **Multi-Protocol Monitoring:** Continuously tracks yields across Frax, Aave, Compound, and more
2. **Risk-Adjusted Analysis:** Considers risk, not just yield (Sharpe ratio, volatility, etc.)
3. **Autonomous Rebalancing:** Automatically reallocates assets when better opportunities arise
4. **AI-Powered Decisions:** Uses ML for better decision-making and strategy adaptation
5. **24/7 Operation:** Operates continuously without human intervention
6. **Safety Limits:** User-defined limits on transaction sizes and risk exposure

**Value Proposition:**
- **For Users:** Maximize yields with minimal effort, risk-aware optimization
- **For Market:** First autonomous multi-protocol risk-adjusted optimizer
- **For Investors:** Clear revenue model, scalable, large market opportunity
